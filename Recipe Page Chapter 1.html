Chocolate Cake Recipe

This delicious chocolate cake is soft, rich, and perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or dessert lovers. It is easy to make and tastes amazing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a bowl.
  3. Add sugar, eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Stir the mixture until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a baking tray.
  6. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool before serving.
