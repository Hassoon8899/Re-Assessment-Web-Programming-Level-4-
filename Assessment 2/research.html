Research Areas

Six interdisciplinary themes where Nexus concentrates its highest-impact research activity.

01

Climate & Environment

Atmospheric science, ocean systems, biodiversity, and evidence-based climate policy at global scale.

02

Health & Longevity

Cancer biology, dementia, infectious disease, and the translation of discoveries into treatments.

03

Digital Society & AI

Machine learning, digital ethics, cybersecurity and the social impact of emerging technologies.

04

Justice & Governance

Human rights law, global institutions, inequality, and democratic accountability.

05

Future Energy Systems

Hydrogen, fusion, battery storage, smart grids, and the economics of decarbonisation.

06

Mind, Brain & Behaviour

Cognitive neuroscience, developmental psychology, language acquisition and mental health.
