Campus Life

175 acres of award-winning campus in the heart of Oxford. Sport, culture, community, and everything in between.

Facilities

Everything You
Need to Thrive

Nexus Library

Over 1.2 million volumes, 24/7 study spaces, 300 PC workstations, and access to 80,000 online journals. The university library is open round the clock during term.

Sports & Wellness Centre

Olympic-standard facilities including a 25-metre swimming pool, eight-lane athletics track, gymnasium, climbing wall and squash courts. Free for all enrolled students.

Dining & Social Spaces

Four on-campus cafés, a main dining hall, and two student-run eateries serving 30+ dietary options. Welcoming social spaces designed for collaboration and community.

Arts & Culture Centre

A 500-seat performance theatre, two gallery spaces, recording studios and rehearsal rooms. Home to student theatre groups, orchestras, and regular visiting artist residencies.
